Mini Player Restorer
Extension Active
How it works
This extension automatically adds the missing mini player button back to YouTube's video controls.
  • Restores the mini player button
  • Works with i keyboard shortcut
  • Matches YouTube's design
  • Auto-updates on page navigation
Usage
Click the mini player button in YouTube's control bar or press i to activate the mini player.
